What is Computer Engineering?

Computer Engineering is a field that combines computer science and electrical engineering to create powerful computer systems. It focuses on both the *hardware* (physical components) and *software* (programming) of computers.

Some key areas in Computer Engineering include:

  • Programming & Software Development– Writing code to create applications, websites, and systems.
  • Microprocessors & Embedded Systems – Designing small computer chips used in smart devices.
  • Networking & Cybersecurity* – Securing networks from hackers and protecting sensitive data.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Robotics– Teaching computers how to think and make decisions.
  • Hardware Design* – Building circuits and developing new computer technologies.
